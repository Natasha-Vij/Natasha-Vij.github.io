Section 5: Fastest Growing Nations

In this section, it is clear to see that China, India, Korea, and the United States grew their healthcare and education spending between 2012 and 2016.

It's also interesting to see that both Japan dropped its healthcare spending significantly in dollars and the United States dropped in military spending. It's also worth noting that China increased their military spending fairly significantly.

This section shows the fastest growth rate in the education, healthcare, and military sectors. It's clear to see that China leads in growth percent increase, followed by India, South Korea, and Indonesia.

It is equally interesting to see how countries like Russia, Canada, Japan, and South Africa have significantly dropped their growth rate percent between 2012 and 2016.
