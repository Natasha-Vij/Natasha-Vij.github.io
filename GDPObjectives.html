Project by: Natasha Vij

DATS 6401-010

Objective:

The purpose of this project is to compare the spending of all G20 countries over the past 10 years. To do so, I will be comparing education, healthcare, and military spending to each country's respective GDP spending. The goal of this project will be to see which countries lead and fall back on spending financial resources on vital aspects of their governments.
