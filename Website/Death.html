New Deaths By Month

While hospitalizations are still fairly high, death rates seem to be decreasing significantly. While data from only the first half of April has been collected for this analysis, the data indicates that the general trend will continue to decrease.

Death vs. Total Cases

Examining the relationship between death and total positive cases helps the scientific community understand which nations are seeing highest rates of deaths. There are five nations that stand out in this analysis: Poland, Spain, France, Italy, the United Kingdom, and Bulgaria.
