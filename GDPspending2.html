Analysis of Education, Healthcare, and Military Countries of G20 Countries

Project by: Natasha Vij

DATS 6401-010

Section 4: Military vs. Education vs. Healthcare Total Spending



In this section, you can see how much each country of the G20 spends on military, education, and healthcare. This section shows how each sector compares to each other. It is clear to see that the United States regularly spends more than any other country on all three sectors; however, it is interesting to take notice of other state actors.

Russia, for example, is second in military spending, but is 15th in overall education spending and 13th in overall healthcare spending.

Germany is ranked third in total education spending and fourth in healthcare even though it is ranked ninth in military spending.

It is also interesting to see how Japan ranks in these three sectors. In education, is ranked fifth. In healthcare, it is ranked third. However, in military spending, it is ranked in eighth place.
